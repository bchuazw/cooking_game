# Walkthrough Script - Hawker Mama

A short read-along script for recording the current playable build.

---

> "This is Hawker Mama, a mobile cooking game teaching Singaporean food through a browser-based voxel kitchen."

*Open the QR with a phone camera, or paste the preview URL on desktop. Resize the browser to phone width if recording from desktop.*

> "The current playable dish is Hainanese Chicken Rice. The old Laksa and Prata material is draft content, but only chicken rice is wired into the build today."

*Tap the Hainanese Chicken Rice start button.*

> "The order is a station workflow. First collect rice from the pantry, then start the rice cooker."

*Move to the pantry, pick up rice, then move to the rice cooker.*

> "Next, prep the chicken at the fridge and cutting board, then poach it in the stock pot."

*Move through fridge, board, and pot.*

> "The chili sauce comes from the mortar, then everything is assembled at the plate station."

*Collect chili sauce, plate rice and chicken, then serve.*

> "The result screen shows the rating, then the food-learning layer can carry the cultural context."

*Show the rating screen and culture-card path.*

---

## Verification

`npm run test:smoke` automates this flow in mobile Chromium and checks stars, FPS, and nonblank Three.js pixels.
