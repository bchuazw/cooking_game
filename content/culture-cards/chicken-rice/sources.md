## Pitfall checks

- ✅ Hainanese origin acknowledged (claim 1, 4).
- ✅ Singapore evolution explicitly named, not credited solely to either side.
- ✅ Sauce convention named (ginger / chilli / dark soy), not flattened to "soy sauce".
- ❌ Halal: this dish uses chicken only; pork is not in classical Hainanese chicken rice. Halal-compatible by default.

## Conflict resolution

Sources differ on whether the rice should be cooked with pandan; we leave that
out of the gameplay step (which uses pandan in the **aromatics** step) and
mention it in the body without prescribing a single canonical method.
