# Chili Crab

Chili crab is credited to Cher Yam Tian, Singapore, 1956. (Hooi Kok Wai also claims authorship — the Infopedia entry holds Cher Yam Tian as primary.) A sweet-spicy sauce of tomato and sambal, finished with egg ribbons. Mop with mantou — leave nothing.

## Did you know?
Sweetness and umami carry it more than heat. Crab tomalley goes back into the sauce.

## Halal note
As a shellfish dish, halal status varies by school: permissible in Maliki/Shafi'i/Hanbali rulings, not in classical Hanafi. The recipe contains no pork or alcohol.
