## Pitfall checks

- ✅ Origin claim (Cher Yam Tian, 1956) sourced from primary Infopedia entry, not folklore.
- ✅ Sauce profile correctly described — sweet/savoury with controlled heat, not "fiery".
- ✅ Mantou ritual mentioned.
- ❌ Halal: crab is shellfish — generally **not halal** in Sunni Hanafi schools, **halal** in Maliki / Shafi'i / Hanbali rulings. We flag this in the dish-specific halal note. The shipped recipe contains no pork or alcohol.

## Conflict resolution

The originator claim is well-sourced via Infopedia. We use it without
hedging in the body card; the alternate folklore (Hooi Kok Wai also claims
authorship) is footnoted in `card.en.md`.
