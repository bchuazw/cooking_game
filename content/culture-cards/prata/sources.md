## Pitfall checks

- ✅ Tamil migrant lineage explicit; relationship to Indian parotta named without claiming origin.
- ✅ Variants (egg / cheese / onion) noted, not flattened.
- ✅ Halal: prata is typically halal at Indian-Muslim mamak stalls. The shipped recipe is halal-compatible (no animal-derived shortening).

## Conflict resolution

Sources differ on the spelling ("prata" / "paratha" / "parotta"). We use
"prata" per common Singaporean usage and mention "parotta" as the cousin term.
